High-Energy Safety Tips

  • Share your route and ETA with trusted contacts before heading out.
  • Prefer brighter roads and active streets over isolated shortcuts.
  • Keep your phone charged and your essential numbers ready to dial.
  • Trust instinct fast. If something feels off, change direction immediately.
  • Stay aware of vehicle details before entering a cab or rideshare.
  • If followed, move toward a shop, hospital, police point, or busy street.
  • Keep one ear free in quieter areas so you can hear what is around you.
  • A whistle, torch, or personal alarm can create a fast attention burst.
